About

Outcome-based recruiting for early stage founders.

For the past decade I've worked at the intersection of high growth technology companies and team building, sourcing and placing enduring leaders and individual contributors into go-to-market and technical roles.

My perspective is unique. I was the first in-house recruiter at Whatnot, was on the go-to-market executive talent team at Sequoia Capital, and started my career in retained executive search. Most recently, I've partnered with Success Venture Partners to support S-Tier AI startups with go-to-market recruiting as a solo recruiter. I'm a hands-on operator who's lived high-growth recruiting from every angle.

I work selectively, source intelligently, am paid only on outcomes, and stay close to the problem longer than most.

Methodology
pirl pitch in real life

Resumes don't capture communication and conviction. 60 seconds of video does.

pirl delivers signal to founders and hiring managers up front, in minutes. Candidates record three short videos: a 60-second intro, a career highlight, and how they're using AI. I manually curate the best matches to put forward, dramatically reducing time spent meeting candidates who aren't a fit.
